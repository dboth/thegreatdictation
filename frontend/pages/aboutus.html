Here we tell you about the great team behind "The Great Dictation":

Once upon a time there was a software project from subject of computional linguistics at Karl-Ruprecht-University Heidelberg.
Four wonderful and creative students got a project idea about how to make learning with dictation awesome for foreigners,
so they started to research and program and design this whole platform.
And if they didn't finished this great project they're still working on it.
