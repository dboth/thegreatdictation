Auditive dictation like ours and therefore listening comprehension will help you by playing a key role in learning a foreign language - while training with this dictation platform your brain will automatically learn

  • to discriminate between sounds,
  • vocabulary and grammatical structures,
  • to interprete stress and intonation for interpunction.

Listening skills (very important for daily life!) get developed and you can concentrate on internalizing the heard grammar rules.
Don't care about producing your own text intonations aka speaking- no additional pressure while handling and improving all the before mentioned processes in your head! (Vandergrift,1999).

Like we said: With dictation you do not only learn vocabulary, you also learn grammar while memorising meaning and relationship of words (Alkire,2002) and you will get some useful standard phrases.

Afterwards you can review a list of your syntactic mistakes, get some advices which learning material you should repeat to get better and you also keep track of your progress (Kazazoglu, 2012).
